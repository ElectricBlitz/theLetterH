Why?

It all started with the 7th letter

Remember Markiplier - Lord Farquad "E?" I thought it would be funny to start "H." Except, it was just the letter. You would reply to someone with just the letter h. It's a form of trolling. I have the world's worst personality and have latched onto it. I don't understand why. Lately, it's just a part of my identity. Not necessarily a humorous thing.

It's just turned into this thing where h = funny. Honestly, I don't even use it often anymore. I just like bringing it up to see what people think. Usually, it's just weird looks.
